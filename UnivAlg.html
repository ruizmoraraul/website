Universal algebra

I did a Bachelor in Mathematics at the University of Valencia. As part of it I collaborated with the department of Mathematics (concretely, the area of algebra) under the tutoring of Enric Cosme Llópez.

When I expressed Enric my interests about the algebraics aspects of logic he proposed me to work in an introduction in universal algebra . Concretely, the course I lectured (18 h) was thought as an introduction to this field with three objectives in mind: the isomorphism theorems, Birkhoff theorem about varieties and Mal'cev conditions.

The lectures were streamed though youtube and can be found in the personal youtube channel of Enric Cosme (they are in catalan) Lecture videos. Moreover, I wrote some lecture notes which are also avaliable (also in catalan) Lecture notes. At the moment I'm working on an english and more complete version of the lecture notes.
